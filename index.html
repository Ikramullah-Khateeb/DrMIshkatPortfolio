About Me

Bridging Modern Medicine and Holistic Vitality

I am Dr. Mishkat Shehata, a Consultant in Family Medicine and a pioneering voice in the global Lifestyle Medicine movement. My practice is built on a singular conviction: Lifestyle often outweighs genetics. With over 15 years of clinical experience spanning the UK’s NHS and the UAE’s leading healthcare institutions, I bridge the gap between acute clinical care and preventative longevity science. My journey has taken me from the rigorous wards of British hospitals to leading the "Longevity Revolution" in the Middle East.

Currently serving as the Clinical Director for Longevity at PureHealth and Vice President of the Emirates Lifestyle and Longevity Medicine Society, I am dedicated to decoding the biological mechanisms of aging. I help individuals and organizations implement "root-cause" solutions optimizing metabolic health, mental resilience, and physical vitality to reverse chronic disease before it starts.

I hold a dual master's degree in Public Health and Health Service Delivery, alongside my medical degree (MBBS) and membership in the Royal College of General Practitioners (UK). I am also a Diplomate of the International Board of Lifestyle Medicine, ensuring my patients receive care that meets the highest global standards.

About Me

Bridging Academia, Leadership, and Holistic Wellness

I am Dr. Edyta Skibinska, a visionary educator and entrepreneur with over 20 years of experience reshaping the landscape of healthcare management and public health. My journey spans from founding successful ventures in Europe to shaping the future of healthcare leaders in the UAE. Currently serving as an Assistant Professor at the American University in the Emirates (AUE), I am dedicated to 'humanizing healthcare'integrating emotional intelligence and ethical leadership into modern medical education.

Beyond the classroom, I am the Founder of Wealth of Health FZE, where I bridge the gap between clinical management and holistic lifestyle interventions.

I hold a Ph.D. in Medicine and a Master’s in Public Health from the Medical University of Lodz, complemented by a Healthcare Management Certification from the Yale School of Management. I was recently honoured with the 'Outstanding Lifestyle Medicine & Longevity Excellence Award' at the UAE Next Mastermind Awards."
